Hunt by ear

Somewhere in these frames, things are moving. Nobody will tell you where. The sound you hear is computed live from the raw pixels along the track you are guessing — when light from every frame piles up into one sharp point, it sings. The pipeline's answers stay sealed until you commit.

Arrows — move (Shift = big steps)  ·  Space — mark a candidate, then confirm it frame by frame (press space once per frame)
Tab — roam / tune mode  ·  [ / ] — speed down / up (Shift = fine)  ·  ; / ' — rotate direction (Shift = fine)
V — speak current speed & direction  ·  P — speak position  ·  Enter — finish hunting and see your results
Roam mode: the app tries every velocity at your cursor and plays the best honest track it finds. Tune mode: you control speed and direction yourself — the pulse quickens as the stack snaps into focus.
